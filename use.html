How to Use the Image Editing Website

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Visit the Home Page: Go to the Home Page of the website.
  2. Select an Operation: Choose the image processing operation you want to perform from the dropdown menu.
  3. Upload an Image: Click the 'Choose File' button to upload an image from your device.
  4. Submit the Form: Click the 'Submit' button to process your image.
  5. View the Result: Once the image is processed, a link to the edited image will be provided. Click on the link to view and download your edited image.

Available Operations

  • Convert to PNG: Convert your image to PNG format.
  • Convert to JPG: Convert your image to JPG format.
  • Convert to Grayscale: Convert your image to grayscale.
  • Convert to WEBP: Convert your image to WEBP format.
  • Convert to GIF: Convert your image to GIF format.
  • Apply Cartoon Effect: Apply a cartoon effect to your image.
